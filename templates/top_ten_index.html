What are the deadliest cancers?

According to the WHO, the three cancers that killed the most people worldwide in 2020 were lung cancer (1.8M deaths), colorectal cancer (916k deaths) and liver cancer (830k deaths). And prostate cancer and breast cancer are among the most common types. But those aren't the deadliest cancers.

The deadliest cancers are those with the lowest survival rates.

which cancer is the most dangerous?

Only about 11.5% of patients with pancreatic cancer are alive five years after their diagnosis. This is the lowest survival rates for any kind of cancer and makes the pancreatic cancer the most deadliest.
